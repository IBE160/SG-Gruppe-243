Theme Personality

This "Classic Professional" theme conveys **trust, reliability, and competence**. The primary blue instills confidence, while the clean grays provide a solid, no-nonsense foundation. The subtle accent offers visual warmth without being distracting, perfectly aligning with an application that helps users feel **Empowered, Ready, and Professional**. Ideal for tools in finance, business, or career development.
