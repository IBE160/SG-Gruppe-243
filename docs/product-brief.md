# Product Brief: AI CV & Job Application Assistant

## 1. Project Overview

**Project Title:** AI CV & Job Application Assistant

## 2. Purpose

The core purpose of this application is to empower students and recent graduates in their job search journey. It aims to help them generate customized job applications by leveraging their existing CVs, previous applications, keywords, and target job descriptions. The tool will significantly improve application quality by suggesting edits to both the CV and the application letter, identifying missing qualifications, and optimizing applications for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

## 3. Target Audience

*   University and college students seeking internships or entry-level positions.
*   Recent graduates looking to improve their job application materials and increase their chances of securing interviews.

## 4. Key Problem Solved

Students and job seekers often struggle to effectively tailor their applications to specific job postings, leading to generic submissions that are frequently overlooked. This tool addresses the time-consuming and often complex task of customizing application materials, providing an AI-powered solution to enhance relevance and impact.

## 5. Core Value Proposition

"Your AI Career Co-pilot: Get the interview you deserve. Tailor every application with AI precision, identify skill gaps, and boost your confidence in the Norwegian job market."

## 6. Key Features (MVP)

*   **Personalized Application Generation:** Automatically creates tailored application letters.
*   **CV Improvement Suggestions:** Provides AI-driven recommendations for enhancing CV content.
*   **Qualification Gap Analysis:** Identifies missing skills relative to job requirements.

## 7. Future Considerations

*   **ATS Optimization:** Further refinement of content and formatting for Applicant Tracking Systems.
*   **Language & Tone Customization:** Options for adjusting writing style and language level.
*   **Career Exploration & Mock Interviews:** AI-powered features for broader career development.

## 8. Success Metrics

*   Increased user satisfaction and engagement.
*   Measurable improvement in interview callback rates for users.
*   High accuracy and helpfulness of AI-generated suggestions.
*   Adherence to security (GDPR compliance) and performance targets.
